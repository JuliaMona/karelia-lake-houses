About

A calm escape built around the lak

A collection of carefully designed cottages by the lake, where architecture meets nature and every stay feels calm, private and effortless.

Lake Houses Karelia is a collection of private lakeside cottages surrounded by pine forests and natural silence.

Each house is designed as a personal retreat — a place to slow down, disconnect from the city and reconnect with nature in comfort. Large windows, natural materials and warm interiors create a calm and inviting atmosphere all year round. Whether it’s a quiet weekend, a romantic getaway or a longer stay, the experience is built around privacy, simplicity and the beauty of northern landscapes.
